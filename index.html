About Triton

Triton ( Telkom Industrial Hackathon), is a national-wide Internet of Things hackathon competition, as a mean to further improve and inovate our industrial sector with the emergence of IoT-based solution

we challenge you, the indonesia brightest thinker to show off your IoT based solution and inovation to compete against other in these category:

  • Chemistry

  • Small Micro and
    Medium Enterprises

  • Health

  • Home
    Automation

  • Security

  • Government

  • Economy

  • Agriculture
